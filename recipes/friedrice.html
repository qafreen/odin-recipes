Fried Rice

Description

Spicy and delicious fried rice made lots of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger chopped finely
  • 1 tbsp Garlic chopped finely
  • 2 Green Chilli chopped finely
  • 4 Spring Onions white part chopped finely
  • 1 Carrot chopped finely
  • 1 cup Cabbage chopped finely
  • Â½ Bell Pepper chopped finely
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 4 Spring Onion (Green Part chopped finely)
  • 1 cup Basmati Rice

Steps

  1. Cook basmati rice and drain it. Cool it completely.
  2. Heat oil in wok on high heat.
  3. Add in ginger, garlic, spring onions, green chillies and toss well.
  4. Add in veggies, and cook for 5 mins on high heat till tender.
  5. Add in salt, sugar,pepper,soy sauce and vinegar and mix well.
  6. Add in cooked rice and toss well.
  7. Now add some extra pepper powder, spring onion greens and mix well.
  8. Serve hot.
