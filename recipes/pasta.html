Pasta

Description

Simple Tomato Garlic Pasta is a quick and flavorful dish that combines the robustness of tomatoes with the aromatic intensity of garlic. It's an easy-to-make recipe that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner, offering a comforting and satisfying meal with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the tomato sauce.
  5. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley if desired.
