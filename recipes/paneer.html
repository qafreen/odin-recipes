Paneer Butter Masala

Description

A popular random Indian dish is Paneer Butter Masala. It's a rich and creamy tomato-based curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), butter, and a variety of spices. Paneer Butter Masala is a beloved dish in Indian cuisine, known for its rich and flavorful profile.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cream
  • Spices (such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder)
  • Cashews (optional, for extra creaminess)

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes and lightly fry them in a pan until they are golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat some butter and add finely chopped onions. Sauté until they are golden.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste, and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add spices like garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for a few more minutes.
  6. For a smoother texture, you can blend the gravy using a blender. This step is optional.
  7. Return the gravy to the pan, add a bit of water if it’s too thick, and bring it to a boil.
  8. Add the fried paneer cubes and simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Stir in some cream and add a handful of cashews (optional).
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
